Ender Dragon

Health: 100 ❤️

The Ender Dragon is a
formidable boss mob that resides
in the End dimension. It
has black scales, large wings,
and a distinctive purple breath
attack. When summoned, the Ender
Dragon flies around the End
Island, destroying blocks and attacking
players with its powerful breath
attack and physical strikes.
